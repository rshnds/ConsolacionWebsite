Sarok Festival

When: October 15, 2025

The festival showcases the sarok, a traditional hat made from bamboo and dried banana leaves, which symbolizes the town's agricultural heritage.

  • Street Dancing: Participants in colorful costumes inspired by the sarok.
  • Cultural Showcases: Performances highlighting the traditions and lifestyle of the locals.
  • Competitions: Artistic and cultural contests to engage the community.
