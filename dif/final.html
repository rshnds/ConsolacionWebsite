Notable Festival and Events

Sarok Festival

Date: February 14, 2025

  • Street Dancing: Participants in colorful costumes inspired by the sarok.
  • Cultural Showcases: Performances highlighting the traditions and lifestyle of the locals.
  • Competitions: Artistic and cultural contests to engage the community.

Town Fiesta

Date: October 29, 2025

  • Religious processions and masses honoring Saint Narcissus.
  • Festive gatherings with abundant food shared among family and friends.
  • Entertainment such as live music, pageants, and parades.

Cebu Charter Day Event

Date: February 14, 2025

  • Commemoration of Consolacion’s municipal status with civic and cultural activities.
  • Government-organized programs, sports events, and entertainment shows.

Christmas and New Year Events

Date: December 2024 to January 2025

  • Town-wide Christmas lighting displays.
  • Community caroling and holiday events.
  • New Year's Eve fireworks and celebrations.

Holy Week Processions

Date: March 24 to March 30, 2025

  • Devotional processions and reenactments of the Passion of Christ.
  • Visita Iglesia (visiting churches) is popular among devotees in Consolacion.

Semana sa Konsolasyon (Week in Consolacion)

Date: July 1 to July 7, 2025

  • A week-long celebration featuring local talent, products, and cuisine.
  • Trade fairs, exhibits, and community-building activities.
