Greenland Cabins

A rustic simplicity of a campsite where you can camp comfortably and elegantly while still being surrounded by nature.

Exclusive Rates

Perhaps the greatest and closest glamping destination for you is the Greenland Cabins. With contemporary urban amenities like an air-conditioned glamping tent and a stunning swimming pool with a wooden lounge deck and bamboo canopy, it has the rustic simplicity of a campsite.

How to Get There?

Exact Location

By Commute: Ride any PUJ/PUB going to SM Consolacion. You can ride a tricycle or motorcycle to “The Greenland Cabins” from there.

By Car: Navigate with Waze or Google Maps and set your destination to “The Greenland Cabins” in Consolacion. Parking space is available.
