SAROK MAKING

is a traditional woven hat from Consolacion, Cebu. Made from bamboo strips and banana leaves, it's known for its distinct conical shape and craftsmanship. Originally used for protection from the sun and rain, the Sarok symbolizes Consolacion's rich cultural heritage and resilience.

Discover Local Cuisine

Talaba

also known as oysters, is a popular seafood delicacy in Consolacion and other coastal areas of the Philippines. Talaba is typically enjoyed in various ways, including raw, steamed, grilled, or cooked in flavorful dishes.

Lechon Belly

Lechon Belly is a beloved dish in Consolacion, Cebu. Often served during festive occasions and family gatherings, this dish showcases the perfect balance of tender, flavorful pork and a crispy, golden skin.

Dinuguan

This savory stew made from pork, pig's blood, vinegar, and spices is a traditional Filipino dish. In Consolacion, Dinuguan is commonly paired with puto (steamed rice cakes) and enjoyed during family gatherings and festivals.

Popular Dining Spots

Cebu's Original Lechon Belly

is a well-known dining spot in Consolacion, famous for serving lechon belly, a Filipino specialty. The dish features a succulent, tender belly of pork, marinated in a flavorful mix of herbs and spices, then slow-cooked to perfection until the skin is crispy and golden brown.

Lokacion Food Park

Co Jordan Talaba and Bangus Eatery

is a popular dining spot in Consolacion, known for serving fresh and affordable seafood, particularly talaba (oysters) and bangus (milkfish). The eatery is a go-to place for seafood lovers, offering a variety of dishes featuring these local favorites.


Location: 9X8G+QGH, Consolacion, 6001 Cebu

Nanda Café

is a popular spot in Consolacion known for its cozy ambiance and wide selection of affordable meals. The café offers a variety of dishes, with a focus on Filipino comfort food and local specialties.


Location: Brgy, Consolacion, Cebu

Ristorante Tinca

is a well-known restaurant in Consolacion, celebrated for its delicious Italian and Mediterranean-inspired cuisine. The restaurant offers a variety of dishes, including pastas, pizzas, and other classic Italian meals, all prepared with fresh ingredients and authentic flavors.

Tasha's Korean Grill and Restaurant

Tasha's Korean Grill and Restaurant in Consolacion is a popular dining destination known for serving authentic Korean cuisine. The restaurant is famous for its grilled dishes, particularly Korean BBQ, where diners can enjoy grilling their own meat at the table. It offers a variety of Korean dishes such as bulgogi, samgyeopsal (grilled pork belly), bibimbap, and kimchi, all served with flavorful sides.


Location: Purok Bougainvilla, Consolacion, 6001
