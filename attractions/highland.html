Highland Mango Ecofarm

The Highland Mango Ecofarm is perfect for visitors to relax and chill out. Take a trek in the forest, swim in the infinity pool, or simply breathe in the pure mountain air.

How to Get There?

Exact Location

By Commute: Take a jeepney with route number 62B heading to Pit-os and alight at Flying V Gasoline Station, the last stop for this route. From there, you can hire a habal-habal driver to take you to Highland Mango Ecofarm. Fare is ₱100 to ₱150 per person.

By Car: Navigate with Waze or Google Maps and set your destination to “Highland Mango Ecofarm” in Consolacion. Spacious parking is available.
