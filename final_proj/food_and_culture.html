SAROK MAKING

is a traditional woven hat from Consolacion, Cebu. Made from bamboo strips and banana leaves, it's known for its distinct conical shape and craftsmanship. Originally used for protection from the sun and rain, the Sarok symbolizes Consolacion's rich cultural heritage and resilience.
